AI Tools & Workflow Assessment

Initial assessment: $999.

The assessment gives you a plain-English view of where time is being lost, which fixes are realistic, and what should happen next.

  • Short intake and discovery before the call
  • 45-minute discovery and assessment call
  • Review of workflow friction, pain points, tools, and transcripts or notes when available
  • Practical recommendations sorted by value, effort, cost, risk, and owner involvement
  • Short report or deck with quick wins and follow-on options

Not an AI audit for its own sake.

The point is not to buy more tools. The point is to get time back from stuck work.

Sometimes the answer is AI-assisted drafting. Sometimes it is a cleaner intake form, a better handoff, a follow-up rhythm, or a tool you already own finally being used correctly.

Follow-on options after the assessment.

Optimize

Clean up existing workflows, templates, handoffs, forms, documentation, and tool habits so the business runs with less repeated friction.

Automate

Build simple, useful automations where they are safe: intake, reminders, follow-up drafts, status updates, reporting, and recurring admin work.

System improvements / implementation support

Scoped support to put approved recommendations into practice. Follow-on work is priced separately once the scope is clear. Cost depends on complexity, tool risk, owner involvement, and how much implementation help is actually needed.
