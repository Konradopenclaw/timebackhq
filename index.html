Start with the work that is actually stuck.

Most businesses do not lose time in one dramatic failure. They lose it in the small repeated friction that quietly becomes normal: missed follow-ups, unclear handoffs, scattered notes, late invoices, and software that never became a real system.

  • Follow-ups living in inboxes, texts, calendars, and memory
  • Customer updates that depend on whoever remembered last
  • Notes and project details scattered across tools
  • Invoices, estimates, or next steps sent later than intended
  • Software that exists but is not part of a clean operating rhythm

Workflow & AI Opportunity Assessment

The assessment looks at where time is being wasted, what can be simplified, and which fixes are safe enough to try first. Sometimes that means an AI tool; sometimes it means a cleaner handoff, a better intake form, or a process your team can actually follow.

  • Discovery focused on workflow friction
  • Workflow friction map
  • Quick-win recommendations
  • Short written assessment report with next-step options
